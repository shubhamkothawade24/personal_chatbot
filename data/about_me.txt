Shubham Kothawade was born on 24th June 2001. He is currently pursuing his MS in Engineering Management. During his engineering, he was actively involved in designing and manufacturing go-karts, electric bikes, and buggies, eventually becoming the chief coordinator.

Shubham worked at Kavruk Technologies, a startup that supports national and international companies in product development. He also published two research papers in international journals and led several championship-winning teams.

In his free time, he enjoys playing computer games and exploring new hiking trails. He follows a vegetarian diet but eats eggs.

Shubham is currently working on projects related to agile systems and lean manufacturing and is serving as a Scrum Master in his team.
